RULES

  • Use your keyboard left and right arrows to control Bar
  • You can also use the on screen buttons
  • You have 2 lives
  • Directional controls also launches the ball
  • You cant control what speed ball launches at
  • But once ball is launched you can affect balls speed using the bar
  • If bar is not moving when ball hits ,then ball velocity remains unaffected
  • If bar moves in opposite direction while receiving ball ,then ball speed decreases
  • If bar moves in same direction while receiving ball ,then ball speed increases
  • If ball is moving slow enough,moving bar in opposite direction will change ball direction
